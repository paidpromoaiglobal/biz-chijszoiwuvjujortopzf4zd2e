About Us

B2W Unisex Health and Fitness Studio

B2W Unisex Health and Fitness Studio is a top-rated gym located on the 3rd Floor of K Square, NH Bypass Road, Nolambur, Chennai. Since opening its doors, B2W has been committed to delivering a clean, well-equipped, and results-driven fitness environment for men and women of all fitness levels. With a 4.1-star rating and a growing community of loyal members, the studio has earned its reputation through dedicated trainers, modern equipment, and a welcoming atmosphere that keeps members coming back day after day.

At B2W, we believe fitness is more than just lifting weights — it's a lifestyle. Our facility features separate workout zones including a dedicated floor exercise room, a wide range of cardio machines, and hygienic bathrooms maintained to the highest standards. Our expert trainers, including the highly acclaimed Trainer Vishal, provide personalized guidance to help you achieve real, measurable results — whether your goal is weight loss, strength building, sports performance, or overall wellness. Open six days a week from 5:00 AM and on Sundays for morning sessions, B2W fits seamlessly into every schedule.

  • Rated 4.1/5 by 102+ satisfied members with consistently excellent trainer feedback
  • Clean, hygienic facility with separate floor exercise room and well-maintained bathrooms
  • Open 6 days a week from 5:00 AM — early morning to late evening hours to suit every schedule
